about the owner:

I have a total of over 15 years of painting experience including standard brush, roll, preparation work, and spraying. Painting work began during high school summers and continued throughout high school into college

Because of this experience I am able to do projects ranging from simple one room jobs up to three story residential exterior jobs and everything in between.

Living and Working in the lowcountry

I was raised in Mount Pleasant and still live and work in the Lowcountry. I also spent a few years at a local non-profit as the director of operations and was in charge of maintaing the facility which included frequent detailed painting jobs of the exhibit halls and the intricate peices themselves.

About the company

Licensed, Bonded, and Insured

I started this company to bring an honest and fair painting service to Charleston. I hear from many friends and neighbors how hard it is to find or even get in touch with reliable contractors.

I came up with the name of Professional Painting of Charleston by just thinking about what I wanted the company to be. Local, professional, and specific with a focus on quality. I didn't want to do everything just "okay" but wanted to focus on doing one thing really well.

Professional Painting of Charleston's goal is to provide great service at a fair price and to add color to your life.
